Creamy Battle

Creamy Battle is a competitive local multiplayer game from 2 to 4 players or against CPU. In this game your objective is to be the last ice cream of the arena and defeat the other players.

My responabilities:

  • Implemented game mechanics and core programming.
  • Local multiplayer detection with Rewired (licencee).
  • Implemented AI with three levels of difficult based on my revisitation of A* pattern.
  • UI/UX manager.
  • Implemented various tools for game designer like map generator or sound manager.
