Repearth

Global Game Jam Link
What I used:
  • Unity


A monodimensional videogame in which you decide the destiny of the world. You are a volcano that erupts and you have to keep the balance between the live species. Will you annihilate the parasites? Or will you find the perfect equilibrium?

I worked on this project as programmer on Game Jam session in 2020 with Sugni Games Team. The theme of that jam was repair .

You can find informations about this game on jam site -> Repearth.
